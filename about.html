About Our Company

We are a leading transport company with a mission to provide safe, reliable, and comfortable transportation services. Our fleet consists of modern vehicles operated by professional drivers. Riversjoy Travel and Tours Limited is a private-public intercity logistics and transport outfit, incorporated as a limited liability company on November 30, 2020. This “child” of necessity commenced operations December 2, 2020. We are an outstanding NIPOST-Licensed Courier operator in Nigeria; with prospect for international trips. The vision of the ownership and management of the company is to provide quality service (in terms of timely, safe, secured/insured, available, accessible, affordable effective and efficient) logistics and transport services, within and beyond Nigeria. The mission is to deploy professionalism and adequate management mix (human and material resources) in the business operation, from time to time. The shareholders, Directors and management of the company are seasoned Professionals and Practitioners, with many dependable years of experience in the industry. The company has its Administrative Head Office in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. CORE VALUE: Professionalism and proficiency Cost-effectiveness/value addition Discipline/Diligence Safety and security Service customization (Responsiveness) and/or customer-concern Responsibility and Accountability.
